Framepick

Drop in a video — get back its scene-change keyframes, with timestamps. No upload, no server. Everything runs in your browser.

Drop a video here

Or choose a file. It’s decoded on-device with WebCodecs — nothing is uploaded.

This browser doesn’t support the WebCodecs VideoDecoder API, which Framepick needs to decode video on-device. Try a recent Chrome, Edge, or Safari.
